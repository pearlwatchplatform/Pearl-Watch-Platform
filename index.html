Pearl Watch's Breast Cancer Risk & Awareness Calculator

The Breast Cancer Risk and Awareness Calculator is backed by health research to calculate generalized breast cancer risk scores and awareness. Combining demographic, lifestyle, reproductive, and knowledge-based questions, it allows you to pinpoint areas for improvement and gaps in awareness, monitor lifestyle choices, and take proactive steps toward long-term breast health while adopting healthier habits early. This assessment is intented to solely inform you, not diagnose or confirm any present disease.

Terms of Use/Privacy: This calculator serves as an anonymous survey. Results are calculated entirely on your device, and no personal data from the calculator is collected or stored by Pearl Watch.
