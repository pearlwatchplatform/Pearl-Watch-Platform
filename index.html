Breast Cancer Risk & Awareness Calculator

The Breast Cancer Risk & Awareness Calculator is designed to encourage reflection and learning. The scores and categories it generates are based on publicly available research and general health guidelines, but they do not predict or confirm any medical condition. Open to everyone and especially encouraging youth to participate, this survey estimates generalized risk and awareness levels based on your answers so you can learn about healthy habits, and family history.

Terms of Use/Privacy: This calculator serves as an anonymous survey. Results are calculated entirely on your device, and no personal data from the calculator is collected or stored by Pearl Watch.
